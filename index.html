Berlin

Berlin is a very alternative, cultural city with many walks & tours to explore its history. It also has a very vibrant nightlife & is well known for its eccentric nature.

Highlights: Nightlife, Shopping, Museums

Madrid

Madrid is home to the Spanish Royal family as well as the Spanish Government. It is a modern metropolitan city and an economical and industrial center of Spain

Highlights: Nightlife, Culture, Museums

London

London is the capital of England and the United Kingdom. The River Thames travels through the city. London is the biggest city in western Europe, and the world's largest financial centre.

Highlights: Nightlife, Shopping, Finance

Venice

Venice is a city in Italy. It is the capital of the Veneto region, which is in the north-east of the country. It is best known for its romantic Gondola trips & breathtaking canals.

Highlights: Architecture, Romantic getaway, Museums

Paris

Paris (nicknamed the "City of light") is the capital city of France, and the largest city in France. It is a diverse city well known for its historic monuments & fashion.

Highlights: Nightlife, Shopping, Monuments

Athens

Athens became the leading city of Ancient Greece in the first millennium BC. Laying the foundations of Western civilization today, much of its history can still be seen to this day.

Highlights: Architecture, Beaches, History
